Table: products
Schema: SALES
Connection: oracle_10

Description:
Product catalog with inventory and pricing information.

Columns:
- product_id (NUMBER, Primary Key): Unique identifier for each product
- product_name (VARCHAR2(100), NOT NULL): Product name/title
- product_code (VARCHAR2(50), UNIQUE): SKU or product code
- category (VARCHAR2(50)): Product category
- price (NUMBER(10,2), NOT NULL): Current selling price
- cost (NUMBER(10,2)): Cost price for margin calculation
- stock_quantity (NUMBER, DEFAULT 0): Current stock level
- supplier (VARCHAR2(100)): Supplier name
- description (VARCHAR2(1000)): Product description
- weight (NUMBER(8,2)): Product weight in kg
- dimensions (VARCHAR2(50)): Product dimensions (LxWxH)
- is_active (CHAR(1), DEFAULT 'Y'): Product availability (Y/N)
- created_date (DATE, DEFAULT SYSDATE): Date added to catalog
- last_updated (DATE): Last modification date

Indexes:
- PK_PRODUCTS on product_id
- UK_PRODUCT_CODE on product_code
- IDX_PRODUCT_CATEGORY on category
- IDX_PRODUCT_ACTIVE on is_active

Foreign Keys:
None

Related Tables:
- order_items (product_id referenced by)
- product_reviews (product_id referenced by)
- inventory_movements (product_id referenced by)

Example Queries:
-- Get all active products in Electronics category
SELECT * FROM products WHERE category = 'Electronics' AND is_active = 'Y';

-- Find low stock products
SELECT product_name, stock_quantity FROM products WHERE stock_quantity < 10 AND is_active = 'Y';

-- Get product with price range
SELECT * FROM products WHERE price BETWEEN 100 AND 500 ORDER BY price;
